Exercise, an annoying concept to some and an amazing concept to others. However no matter how annoying we may think it is, it's very important for our health. We are recommended to have at least 30 minutes of exercise everyday. Physical activities have many advantages, it can help reduce the risk of heart disease, help your body manage blood sugar and insuline levels, increase your chances of living longer, etc.

Not getting exercise is just as bad as smoking. Absence of physical inactivity heightens chances of major health conditions. Other effects of the absence of exercise are type 2 diabetes, breast and colon cancer, obesity, and more! Approximatly 5.3 million out of 57 millions deaths are caused by physical inactivity(2008).

Exercising does not mean spending large amounts of money on a gym membership or getting a personal trainer, it can be done just from the comfort of your own home. Mordern technology allows us to search up simple, and equipment-less exercise routines that we can do at home. Some activities that can be done at home are push-ups which improves your posture, increase whole body muscle, and prevents lower back injuries. Planking is another easy way of warming up, it healps you reduce belly fat and even getting that six-pack look, it also reduces back pain by strengthen the core. Jumping jacks can be done almost anywhere as well, doing 3 sets of 100 "jacks" throughout the day is preferred. One set of jumping jacks only takes 2 minutes and just by this a easy 60 calories can be burn a day. Squatting is another exercise that can be done anywhere, just by bending over and crouching but not touching the floor is another easy exercise to burn calories. Squatting is mostly known as a leg exercise however it has benefits throughout the entire body by promoting body-wide muscles. Doing 2-3 sets of 20 squats stabilizes muscles and help with waste removal. In summary exercising helps you imporve your body, have more confident in yourself, and most importantly live longer! It can be done almost everywhere not only the gym, many substitutions can make up the equipments taht they have at the gym.
